Methodology

This study investigated the relationship between household energy consumption and socio-economic factors across London boroughs, focusing on the effects of median income while controlling for household size and energy efficiency ratings.

Key Variables

  • Mean gas consumption (kWh/meter)
  • Median income (GBP)
  • Energy efficiency rating (1-100)
  • Mean people per household

Data Sources

  • Department for Energy Security and Net Zero
  • Greater London Authority
  • Office for National Statistics
  • Ministry of Housing, Communities & Local Government
