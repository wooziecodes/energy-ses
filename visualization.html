Relationships Between Variables

The scatterplot matrix below shows the relationships between key variables in our study:

Figure 1: Scatterplot matrix showing relationships between gas consumption, income, household size, and energy efficiency ratings

Key Relationships:

  • Gas Consumption vs Income: Positive correlation showing higher consumption in wealthier areas
  • Household Size vs Consumption: Strong positive relationship indicating larger households consume more gas
  • Energy Efficiency vs Consumption: Negative correlation showing efficiency improvements reduce consumption
