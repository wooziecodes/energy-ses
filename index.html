Methodology

  1. Data Preparation & Validation

    Removed outliers (City of London) and transformed variables to meet statistical assumptions. Square root transformation of income achieved normality (Shapiro-Wilk p=0.060).

  2. Statistical Analysis

    Addressed multicollinearity through variable centering, reducing VIF from >300 to <3.5. Multiple regression with interaction terms revealed complex relationships between variables.

  3. Model Validation

    Comprehensive residual analysis confirmed normality (Shapiro-Wilk p=0.367) and homoscedasticity, ensuring robust results.
